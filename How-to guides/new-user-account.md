
# Create your user account

## Step 1
Go to https://gateway.farearth.space/ 

![Step 1](images/how-to-user-account-1.png)
## Step 2
Click on "Sign up now"

![Step 2](images/how-to-user-account-2.png)

## Step 3
Enter your e-mail address

![Step 3](images/how-to-user-account-3.png)

## Step 4
Click on "Send verification code"

![Step 4](images/how-to-user-account-4.png)

## Step 5
Open your e-mail 

Copy your code

![Step 5](images/how-to-user-account-5.png)

## Step 6
Type in your code

![Step 6](images/how-to-user-account-6.png)

## Step 7
Click on "Verify code"

![Step 7](images/how-to-user-account-7.png)
## Step 8
Type in your password of choice

![Step 8](images/how-to-user-account-8.png)
## Step 9
Click on "Create"

![Step 9](images/how-to-user-account-9.png)

## Step 10
Welcome to *FarEarth*!

![Step 10](images/how-to-user-account-10.png)
